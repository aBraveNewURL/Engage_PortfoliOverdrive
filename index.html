Now, this is a story all about how my life got flipped-turned upside down. And I'd like to take a minute just sit right there I'll tell you how I became the prince of a town called Bel-Air. She gave me a kiss and then she gave me my ticket I put my Walkman on and said, "I might as well kick it". First class, yo this is bad drinking orange juice out of a champagne glass. I hope they're prepared for the prince of Bel-Air.
